ABOUT US

Premo Italiano prides itself on fresh, quality ingredients, passion and flavor; we also take our food very seriously.
Our pizzas spring from a rich family tradition of pie making, dating back to Naples in the 1800's in the old country.
Every chef here is an artisan, and every customer a rightful critic, so we strive to achieve perfection with each pizza we create.
Pizza is more than just food to us here, it's a calling. A calling to create an experience for our customers. A joyful one.
From that calling comes hard-work, craft, and a variety of unique flavors and toppings that we have originated over the decades.
Try our zesty pepperoni, or our luscious white pies, with basil, olive oil and fresh, leafy greens. You won't leave unsatisfied.
In the mood for something different? Sample our award-winning pastrami. Or our fresh-brewed italian coffee with scones.
Come to where the pasion is: Premo Italiano. We live for food, and it shows. Put us to the test.
