Jago and the Tiger Spirit

Jago first entered the scene in 1994, in the seminal fighting classic Killer Instinct. Rare's inaugural arcade offering
ushered in a classic franchise and a new era of fighting mechanics based off chaining complex combos.
Jago entered the Ultra Tech competition led by the Tiger Spirit to defeat Eyedol and his chief minion, Fulgore. With the strength of the tiger, he
stopped Ultratech and helped to free Cinder from bondage at the hands of Eyedol.
Now, years later, the call of the warrior has lead Jago to a new tournament. Who will emerge victorious? Can Jago tame the tiger spirit within?
