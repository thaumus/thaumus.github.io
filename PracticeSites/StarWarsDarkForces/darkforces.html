DARK FORCES

History Re-release Lucasarts

Dark Forces was released in 1995 to great acclaim, both fiscally and critically.
Far ahead of its time as a FPS set in the Star Wars Universe, Dark Forces
would pave the way for games like the modern series Battlefront.
Introducing now classic characters like Kyle Katarn and Jan Oors,
Dark Forces holds a special place in the nerve center of both Star Wars fans
and gamers, new and old.
