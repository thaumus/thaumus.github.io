About the Artist:

BD Isaac has been composing music for indie films since 2004, beginning with the popular underground short based on the Castlevania Series, Prelude to War. In 2008, he created music for Oscar Winning Director Guillermo Del Toro, for Hellboy 2: The Golden Army. Transitioning from rock-based compositions to orchestral, Isaac is currently creating work in the vein of Zimmer and Elfman; dark and complex, yet accessible.
